# valentines-card
This project was originally made during a Special Activites Division (SAD) workshop a while ago. The workshop was hosted by Charlotte and explained how to create a virtual Valentine's Day card in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I added onto it to make a small incremental clicker game for Hack Club: The Game. I was inspired by Universal Paperclips!

To play, click on the envelope. At first, it has two states: closed (needs to be opened and stuffed) and opened (needs to be sealed and sent). <b>The player only gets currency when the envelope is sent, not when stuffed.</b>

There are three shop items so far:

- Automatic Envelope Stuffer (30): Have a machine automatically stuff and seal your envelope! Halves the amount of clicks needed.
- Mailman Intern (50): Hire a brand new mailman intern to send your envelopes to the post office! Increases your currency by 1 each second.
- Carrier Pigeon (100): Hire a very experienced delivery pigeon! Increases your currency by 3 each second.


I am planning the following minigames/options:

- Chance of wax seal stamps being on stock at the store. Using these increases the currency received per click
